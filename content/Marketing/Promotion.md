# CatSnake a cute casual Puzzle-Platformer about Growing and Pushing Boxes
![[Cat Snake/Wiki/Captures/Gifs/2026-06-18/2026-06-18 17.10.03.gif|400]]
It’s a cozy casual puzzle game with strong movement mechanics (inspired by SnakeBird). You control a CatSnake, it’s both a cat and a snake and it can eat and grow to solve puzzles.

Also, imagine squishing a 7m long CatSnake into a cardboard box 📦
I picked up development again 2 weeks ago, built the prototype a few years ago and ported it to Unity
